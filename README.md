# ProTranslator

ProTranslator is a powerful AI-driven extension for Raycast that seamlessly improves, translates, and paraphrases your text across the entire macOS ecosystem. It supports multiple AI providers, including OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek, Groq, or any OpenAI-compatible API, giving you the flexibility to choose the best model for your needs.

## Features

- **Translator / AI Text Assistant**: Select any text on your screen, and let the AI paraphrase, translate, or refine it instantly. You can even run custom prompts or continue writing naturally.
- **Refine Inline**: Magically transform poorly phrased text into natural, native-sounding sentences right where you type, replacing your text directly without opening the Raycast window.
- **Practice Vocabulary**: Save difficult words while translating to your built-in flashcards, and review them later to improve your language skills.
- **Quick Add Word**: Instantly look up a word's definition via AI and save it to your vocabulary list.
- **History**: Never lose a good translation. Access all your previously improved texts with the History command.

## Setup Instructions

1. Go to Raycast Settings (⌘ + ,).
2. Navigate to Extensions -> ProTranslator.
3. Select your preferred **AI Provider** from the dropdown list.
4. Enter your **API Key** for the chosen provider.
   - For *Gemini*: Get your key from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey).
   - For *OpenAI*: Get your key from [OpenAI Dashboard](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys).
   - For *Groq*: Get your key from [Groq Console](https://console.groq.com/keys).
   - For *DeepSeek*: Get your key from [DeepSeek Platform](https://platform.deepseek.com/).
5. If using a **Custom** provider (e.g., local LLM via LM Studio or Ollama), specify your `Custom Base URL` and `Custom Model`.

## Usage Tips

- Assign a hotkey (like `⌥ + T`) to **Translator** to instantly translate or paraphrase selected text from any app.
- Assign a hotkey (like `⌥ + R`) to **Refine Inline** to seamlessly rewrite emails or messages on the fly.
- Use the **Practice Vocabulary** command to revisit words you've learned. You can flip flashcards and mark them as memorized.

## Privacy & Security

Your text is sent securely to the AI provider you configure. No telemetry or analytics are collected by the extension. Your history and flashcards are stored locally on your device.
